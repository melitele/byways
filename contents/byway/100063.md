This gravel road begins on Mirror Lake Scenic Byway (SR-150), six miles south of the Utah/Wyoming border, goes east to China Meadows, and then north to Stateline Reservoir.  Visitors travel through stands of lodgepole pine mixed with aspen, spruce, and fir, and will find many opportunities to view the surrounding peaks of the Uinta Mountains.
