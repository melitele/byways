The Palisade Fruit and Wine Byway is a perfect way to sample Colorado wines year round and amazing peaches and other fresh fruit in season June to September.

The Colorado Department of Agriculture USDA Specialty Crops Block Grant Program has awarded funding to Colorado Association for Viticulture and Enology (CAVE) to develop the byway. It has not been officially designated as a state scenic byway.
