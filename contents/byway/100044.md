Thousand Lake Mountain Road is a 35-mile scenic backway that runs from Fremont through the Cathedral Valley region of Capitol Reef National Park. The route travels through alpine forests, beneath rugged cliffs, and past expansive viewpoints, with opportunities to explore lakes and side roads along the way. While passenger vehicles can often reach the Deep Creek Desert overlook near Snow Lake in good weather, a high-clearance vehicle is recommended for continuing farther into the Reef.

From SR-72, five miles northeast of Fremont, to Baker Ranch Road which connects to Factory Butte Road and Elkhorn Road, which passes through Capitol Reef National Park and connects to Factory Butte.
