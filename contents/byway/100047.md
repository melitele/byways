State Route 153 from Junction on US-89 to the east end of the Beaver Canyon Scenic Byway, then from SR-153 north along Big John Flat Road, Beaver Creek Road, and Kimberly Road to I-70 at Castle Rock, plus the Kent's Lake Loop (Forest Road 137).
