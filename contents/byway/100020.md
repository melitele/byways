Johnson Canyon Road offers a scenic drive through the colorful cliffs and open landscapes of the Grand Staircase region, with highlights including the old Gunsmoke film set about five miles from Highway 89, whose weathered buildings can still be viewed from the road despite being on private land. The route passes quiet farms, grazing fields, and dramatic White Cliffs rising above juniper and sage-covered terrain, with numerous pullouts and side roads providing opportunities for photography, exploring remote backcountry scenery, and mountain biking on accessible two-track trails.

Popular with families seeking a comfortable way to experience the region’s rugged beauty, the route is also especially favored by motorcyclists because of its light traffic and long, peaceful stretches through the canyon landscape.
